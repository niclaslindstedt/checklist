# Achievements

Every feature in the app is also an unlockable trophy. As you use it — adding a note, archiving a finished item, switching themes, connecting the cloud — you quietly earn achievements that mark how far you've grown into the app. They're sorted into four tiers that climb with you: **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, **Pro**, and **Expert**.

You don't chase them; they find you. The moment an action unlocks something new, the trophy button in the header lights up with a badge.

## How it works

1. **Use the app as you normally would.** Each feature you reach for the first time unlocks its matching trophy in the background.
2. **Watch the header trophy.** When you have new, unacknowledged unlocks it shows a badge. Tapping it then opens a notification listing just the ones you've earned, and closing that clears the badge.
3. **Browse the full tour.** When there's nothing new waiting, the same trophy button opens a guided tour of the whole catalog — every trophy across all four tiers, locked and unlocked, so you can see what's still ahead.

Your progress isn't tied to one device. When you turn on [cloud sync](feature:cloud-sync), the trophies you've earned travel with you, so the tour looks the same on your phone as it does on your laptop.
