# Deadline reminders

The app has always let you give an item a due date, but a due date only
helps if something tells you about it. On the native app, deadline
reminders do exactly that: a local notification when a dated item comes
due, fired by your phone whether or not the app is open.

## How it works

Give an item a due date the usual way (and, if it repeats, a repeat
interval). From then on the app quietly keeps your phone's reminders in
step with your lists. Check an item off, delete it, or edit its date —
even on another device that syncs the change in — and the reminders
rearrange themselves to match. You never schedule a reminder by hand;
having a due date is enough.

Because the reminders live in the operating system, they fire even when
the app is closed. Tap one and it opens the app on the list the item
belongs to.

## When you get reminded

By default a reminder arrives on the morning the item is due. In
Settings you can add more lead time, so one deadline can nudge you more
than once:

- **On the due day** — the morning it's due.
- **The day before** — a day's notice.
- **A week before** — for the ones you want to prepare for.

Turn on as many as you like; each raises its own notification.

## Repeating deadlines

A repeating item — "every 2 weeks", "every month" — reschedules itself.
The app arms the next few occurrences ahead of time, so even if you don't
open it for a cycle or two, the upcoming reminders are already waiting.
Each time you do open it, the window tops back up.

## Permission, and turning it off

The first time you give an item a due date, your phone asks whether the
app may send notifications — not on first launch, when you've no reason to
say yes. Allow it and reminders start; decline and nothing is scheduled.

Reminders are on by default. To stop them entirely, switch **Deadline
reminders** off under Settings → General → Notifications; every scheduled
reminder is cancelled at once. This setting only appears on the native
app — the web version can't send notifications and never asks.
