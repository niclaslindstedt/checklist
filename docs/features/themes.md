# Appearance and theming

The header menu opens a **Settings** dialog, and its **Appearance** tab is
where you make `checklist` look the way you want. The app is dark by
default, but you are not stuck with it.

Pick from **eleven theme presets** — light and dark variants in a range of
palettes — or choose **Custom** and dial in your own colours, corner
radius, density, border width, and motion. Set one of **four fonts**, and
nudge the **text size** up or down until reading and tapping feel right.
Every change applies the instant you make it, so you can preview a look
without leaving the dialog.

## Themed dropdowns

The dropdowns in Settings — the font picker, the text-size picker, and the
log-level filter — are not your browser's native menus. They are custom
pickers styled to match the rest of the app, so a list of options looks
the same on every device. They are fully keyboard-navigable: open one and
use the **arrow keys** (or **Home** / **End**) to move the highlight,
**Enter** or **Space** to choose, and **Escape** to close without changing
anything. The current value carries a check mark.
