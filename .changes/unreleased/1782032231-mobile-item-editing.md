title: Editing items on mobile
---

Editing an item on a phone now keeps the row lined up with the rest of the list, opens the editor from a tap anywhere along the row (not just on the text), moves editing straight to another item when you tap it without the keyboard dropping or the add button flashing back, lets you tick its checkbox while editing, scrolls it into view above the keyboard only when it's actually hidden, and without the page or its frozen header jerking, hides the add button while you type, eases the note field open, and leaves only the system keyboard bar on screen instead of stacking a second one over it.
